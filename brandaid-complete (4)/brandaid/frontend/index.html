About the Event

WHAT IS
Avyaya ?

We believe fashion is a mirror. It shouldn't just cover; it should reveal. Whether it’s the intricate weave of a suit or the delicate curve of an anti-tarnish cuff, our collection is designed to be an extension of your inner grace. You don't just wear Avyaya; you inhabit it.


The Avyaya Collection: A Symphony of Grace At the heart of Avyaya lies a commitment to integrity, tradition, and the pursuit of a sophisticated life. Our curated segments are more than just products; they are the building blocks of an exquisite lifestyle designed to harmonize with your inner grace. Guided by our vision to redefine everyday fashion through conscious creativity, we bring you a versatile range that spans from heritage textiles to modern artisanal decor. Each piece is selected to accentuate your individuality while offering a classic elegance that remains accessible. As we continue to grow, our collection remains a living canvas—purposefully designed to evolve with your needs. We leave a constant window open for new, captivating arrivals that will continue to enhance your routine and elevate your surroundings, ensuring that the Avyaya experience is one of perpetual discovery and timeless style.

Our Impact Direction

SDG
GOALS

Avyaya is built on the belief that true elegance is inseparable from responsibility. Our brand strategy is meticulously aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), ensuring that as we curate a sophisticated life for you, we protect the world we share. Core Pillars of Impact

01

No Poverty

We provide a platform for local artisans and small-scale vendors, ensuring fair trade and economic visibility for traditional craftsmen

02

Good Health & Well-being

We advocate for a "Slow Living" philosophy. By promoting Sanctuary Aesthetics and mindful consumption, we provide tools that reduce environmental stressors and enhance mental well-being. Our focus on anti-tarnish, hypoallergenic materials ensures that our products are as safe for your body as they are beautiful.

03

Quality Education

As a university-born initiative, Avyaya serves as a living laboratory for experiential learning. We bridge the gap between academic theory and industry practice, offering workshops on traditional crafts and modern digital branding to empower the next generation of creative entrepreneurs.

04

Life on Land

Our soul lies in nature. By prioritizing natural handloom fibers and biodegradable materials like air-dry clay, we actively reduce the chemical footprint on terrestrial ecosystems. We support artisanal practices that preserve local biodiversity rather than depleting it through mass industrialization.

05

Partnerships for the Goals

Avyaya operates as an Umbrella Brand, specifically designed to foster a collaborative ecosystem. We unite local vendors, wholesale suppliers, and student creators under one vision, proving that collective action is the most powerful tool for sustainable market transformation.

06

Gender Equality

We provide a platform for local artisans and small-scale vendors, ensuring fair trade and economic visibility for traditional craftsmen.

07

Decent Work & Economic Growth

Our model supports the local "Orange Economy" by creating a sustainable supply chain that values human labor over automated mass production.

08

Sustainable Cities & Communities

We celebrate regional heritage (like Dehradun’s local artistry), keeping traditional cultural identities alive within modern urban lifestyles.

Quick Answers

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

Who can participate? +
Any currently enrolled student of BBA, MBA, B.Com, or any management programme. First-year students are absolutely welcome!
Is there a registration fee? +
Yes, a nominal fee of ₹200 per team. This covers event materials, certificates, and refreshments on the day.
Can first-year students join? +
Absolutely! Brand Aid is designed to be accessible to all levels. First-year students often bring the freshest ideas.
What is the submission format? +
Poster as JPG/PNG (min 1080×1080px), video as MP4 (max 90 seconds, 1080p), marketing plan as PDF (max 5 pages).
Is online participation allowed? +
Round 1 and Ad Submission can be done online. Rounds 3 and 4 require in-person attendance at the venue.
How many members per team? +
Teams of 2 to 4 members. Individual participation is not allowed — collaboration is core to Brand Aid.
